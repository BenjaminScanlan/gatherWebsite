Gather is a dream become reality; a manifestation of those ageless wonders that reside in us all: light, time, vibration and transformation. The four-piece Los Angeles collective -consisting of percussionists Ben Scanlan and Kevin Moran, vocalist Susana Pineda and saxophonist Nicolas Maldonado - aim to offer the transformative power of real magic to the world through dance, sound and color.
